About Charles "Tank" Harris

Charles began his financial services career in 1997 with Smith Barney after graduating from Temple University.

He built a practice that managed over $100M of assets for professional athletes, universities, foundations, and small business owners. He founded The Sports & Entertainment financial group in 2001 and sold the company in 2012.

He partnered with New York Life Insurance Company to build an affordable investment and insurance program for people who had previously been shut out of the financial services industry.

In 2019 he branched out on his own to form the Financial Planning firm, Wealth Our Way.

When it comes to creating a detailed plan that meets your financial goals, there is simply no one better to ask for advice and guidance.
